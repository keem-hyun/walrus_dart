## 0.1.0

* Singleton pattern with `WalrusClient.initialize()` and `WalrusClient.instance`
* Basic blob operations: `store()`, `read()`, `exists()`, `getBlobUrl()`
* Support for `deletable` and `permanent` blob options
* Custom exceptions: `WalrusException`, `StoreException`, `BlobNotFoundException`, `NetworkException`
